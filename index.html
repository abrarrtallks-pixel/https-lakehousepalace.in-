Our Story

A Sanctuary on
Nigeen's Shores

Lake House Palace (by J.V Resort) sits gracefully on the banks of Nigeen Lake — one of Kashmir's most pristine and tranquil waters. Born from a love of nature and a deep-rooted Kashmiri hospitality, we have crafted a retreat that honours the valley's timeless beauty.

Whether you arrive seeking the golden hues of a Dal sunrise, the crisp mountain air of a Himalayan afternoon, or simply the gentle lapping of lake waters outside your window — this is your home away from home.
