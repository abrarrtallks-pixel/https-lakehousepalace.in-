Our Story

Who We Are

Born from a Love of
Kashmir's Waters

Lake House Palace was founded with a single, profound belief: that the shores of Nigeen Lake deserve a place where guests can fully surrender to the valley's unmatched serenity. Our resort was built not just as a business — but as an act of devotion to the land, the lake, and the deeply rooted traditions of Kashmiri hospitality.

Nestled at Barhaall, Nigeen Ghat No.2, we sit on one of Srinagar's most pristine lakefronts. Nigeen Lake — quieter than Dal, more intimate, less touched — has a quality of stillness that can only be experienced. It is this stillness that defines everything we do.

Over the years, we have welcomed guests from across India and around the world — honeymooners, families, solo travellers, and those simply seeking a pause from the relentless pace of modern life. Each one has left carrying a piece of Kashmir's soul.

Our Values

What Drives Us

Three pillars guide everything at Lake House Palace (by J.V Resort) — from how we greet you at the gate to how we say farewell.

🤲

Genuine Hospitality

In Kashmir, a guest is considered God's gift. We carry this philosophy in every interaction — every warm cup of Kahwa, every smile at the ghat, every quiet conversation by the lake.

🌿

Respect for Nature

Nigeen Lake is our home and our responsibility. We practice sustainable hospitality — minimising waste, supporting local suppliers, and protecting the fragile ecosystem we call home.

🏺

Living Heritage

Kashmiri crafts, cuisine, and culture are woven into every corner of our resort. We celebrate artisans, preserve traditions, and share the richness of this ancient valley with every guest.

Why Stay With Us

The Lake View Experience

From the first shimmer of dawn on the water to the stars reflecting in its depths — a stay here is a gift you give yourself.

🌅

Sunrise Over Nigeen

Wake before dawn and witness the Himalayas turn gold as the sun rises over Nigeen's mirror-calm waters.

🛶

Shikara Rides

Step onto a traditional carved Shikara boat and drift through the lotus-filled waters — peaceful beyond words.

🏔️

Himalayan Backdrop

Every room and terrace frames the Pir Panjal mountains — a living painting that changes with every hour of light.

🍛

Kashmiri Wazwan

A full Wazwan feast is one of life's great dining experiences. Savour it lakeside at our in-house restaurant.

🧣

Local Craft Walks

We arrange guided walks through Kashmiri artisan quarters — papier-mâché, Pashmina weaving, woodcarving.

🌺

Garden Tours

Discover Mughal Gardens — Shalimar, Nishat, Chashme Shahi — each a testament to Kashmir's regal history.
