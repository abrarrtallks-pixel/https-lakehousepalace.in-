Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I confirm a booking? +

Simply send us a WhatsApp message with your name, preferred dates, number of guests, and room preference. We'll confirm availability and share payment details within a few hours.

Is breakfast included? +

Breakfast is included with Super Deluxe, Family Suite, and Lake Suite bookings. For Deluxe Rooms, breakfast can be added for an additional charge. Half and full board packages are available for all rooms.

Can you arrange Shikara rides and tours? +

Absolutely! We can arrange private Shikara rides on Nigeen Lake, guided Mughal Garden tours, old city walks, Gulmarg day trips, and more. Just let us know your interests when you reach out.

What is the best season to visit? +

Kashmir is magical year-round. Spring (April–June) brings tulips and blooming chinar. Summer (July–August) is warm and perfect for lake activities. Autumn (September–November) is our personal favourite — golden chinar, crisp air, and harvest colours. Winter (December–February) offers snow-draped landscapes for those who enjoy the cold.

Is airport / railway pickup available? +

Yes, we can arrange comfortable pickup from Srinagar Airport or the railway station. Please let us know your arrival details in advance and we'll ensure a smooth transfer to the resort.
